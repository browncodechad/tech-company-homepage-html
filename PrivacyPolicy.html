Privacy Policy

Our Core Privacy Commitment

At Tech, privacy is one of our top most priorities for our users' experience. We build our products with your pleasure and satisfaction in mind, and keeping your data secure is part of keeping you happy with us and for you to keep trusting us.

Information we collect

We only collect basic data that you provide that is emails, usernames and names etc. Other than that we do not spy on our customers and the only means we get more data is anonymous data collection using various means such as cookies.

Why we keep your data

We keep your data to:

  • Analyse usage patterns to understand our users
  • Improve our products based on those patterns and feedback
  • To communicate with you (If need be)

Third-Party sharing

Your data is only shared with trusted companies and services (like analytics and hosting providers) who are forced to comply with our privacy policies. The data is solely shared to keep our services running and to turn the user data into valuable information.

Your control

At any given moment in time, you can

  • opt out of the data collection policy
  • turn off the cookies in your browser
